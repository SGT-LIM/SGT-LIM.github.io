### Abstract
This paper introduces FUS (Find Undetectable Sound), a machine learning application designed to indicate the direction of undetectable sounds. The application leverages advanced machine learning techniques to enhance the accuracy of sound direction detection, making it a valuable tool in various auditory applications.

### Summary
- **Objective:** Develop a machine learning application to detect the direction of undetectable sounds.
- **Method:** Utilizes machine learning algorithms to enhance sound direction detection accuracy.
- **Results:** Improved detection of sound direction, valuable for auditory applications.
