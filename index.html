Youngsun Lim

Hello :) I am Youngsun Lim, a Master's student at KAIST AI Computer Vision and Machine Learning Lab. My research interests lie in Responsible, Reliable & Safe AI, within the domains of Multimodal and Generative AI. I am a researcher who is constantly mindful of the societal impacts of my work, respecting minorities and striving to make a positive contribution to the world.
