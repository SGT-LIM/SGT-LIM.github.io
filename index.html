Research Experiences

Responsible, Reliable & Safe AI / Generative AI

  • Master’s Research Assistant at CVML Lab, KAIST AI (Aug 2023 - present)
    • First proposed the concept of “image hallucination” to address the failure of text-to-image models in generating factual information from input prompts, and conducted research on benchmarks, evaluation metrics, and solutions.
    • Investigated dataset distillation to address privacy issues in datasets and reduce training costs.
    • Currently researching uncertainty in multimodal generation as a cause of hallucination, aiming to reduce hallucinations and generate reliable datasets.
  • Undergraduate Researcher at ISDS Lab, Sogang University (Aug 2022 - Jul 2023)
    • Led the first project to build a benchmark for the Korean legal domain and eliminate hallucinations from large language models using chain-of-thought prompting to replicate legal experts’ reasoning.

Human-Computer Interaction & Machine Learning

  • Undergraduate Researcher at Lifestyle Media Lab, Sogang University (Apr 2021 - Jul 2022)
    • Conducted research on a machine learning application to detect undetectable sounds and prevent hearing loss, achieving 73% accuracy in sound localization. Presented my work at HCI Korea 2022 and published two first-author papers.
  • “Wearable Assistive Devices for Deaf-Blind People” Project (Mar 2019 - Jun 2019)
    • Led the project in the Physics Capstone Design I class as a team leader.
    • Developed wearable devices using Arduino and ultrasonic sensors to assist deaf-blind individuals.
